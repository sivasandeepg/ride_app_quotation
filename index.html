Services Offered on the Platform

The platform is a multi-service mobility solution — not just a taxi app. A single customer app and a single driver app (with a dual-mode Captain + Carpool module) power three distinct service lines:

Service 01
🚕 Taxi Ride (People)
On-demand point-to-point passenger rides. Customer books, nearest driver accepts, live tracking, fare calculation, cashless or cash payment.
Service 02
📦 Parcel Delivery
Send packages / documents across the city. Sender & receiver details, package type, proof of delivery, same driver network as taxi.
Service 03 — Signature Feature
🚗 Ride Pooling / Car Sharing (Go-Home Mode)
A car owner travelling to another city or on a daily route can publish their journey (source, destination, date, seats, fare). Other customers travelling on the same route can request to join — the system matches overlapping routes automatically, splits fuel cost, and the car owner earns while travelling. Both the driver app's Carpool module and the customer's Ride Sharing booking flow power this feature.
